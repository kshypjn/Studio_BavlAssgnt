Our CNC Process

What is CNC cutting?

Computer Numerical Control (CNC) cutting is a precision manufacturing process that uses computer-controlled machines to cut materials like wood, metal, and plastic with extreme accuracy.

What's a toolpath?

A toolpath is a set of instructions that tells the CNC machine exactly how to move to create your design. It's created from your design files and specifies cutting depths, speeds, and tool changes.

Our services include:

  • Creating toolpaths from your design files
  • Custom design services if you don't have files
  • Precision CNC cutting on quality materials
  • Optional assembly and delivery
